About the Program

Certificate 3-4 in IT

Instead of reading books and memorizing everything we learn from hands on experience, get what we want from github or other sources online and learn from others. The school also funds what we want to do that is IT related.

At MVHS, our Certificate 3 and 4 Information Technology program goes beyond books and reading. We work with real hardware, configure real networks, build websites from scratch, manage Linux servers (Hosted our own Minecraft server from scratch that is on 24/7), and design databases. All learned thru hands on projects, the internet and practical assessments.


This demo portfolio like website showcases the skills and knowledge we've learned from the program, each of us work best in a specific section instead of all. So we teamed up and made this.
